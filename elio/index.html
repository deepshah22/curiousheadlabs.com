Project overview
Your knowledge, rediscovered.

Elio is a personal knowledge archaeologist — an AI-powered system that continuously excavates, connects, and resurfaces the most valuable information from your digital life, delivered as a curated daily feed.

The problem
01
Knowledge accumulates faster than it can be used. Over years, people build up thousands of notes, saved articles, emails, journals, and ideas across a dozen fragmented tools.
02
Most of it gets forgotten. Valuable insights, recurring ideas, and set goals silently decay — not because they lack value, but because no system surfaces them at the right time.
03
Search doesn't solve it. You can only search for what you remember to look for. The most valuable connections are the ones you don't know to ask about.
04
Existing tools optimize for capture, not retrieval. Notion, Obsidian, Drive — great for storing. None proactively bring your own knowledge back to you.
What Elio does
Daily Knowledge Session
A personalized 30-minute reading plan built from your knowledge base — forgotten notes, saved articles, recurring ideas — each explained in context of today.
Proactive Action Items
AI-generated priority tasks surfaced from emails, todos, long-term goals, and deferred items. Identifies what matters now, not just what was most recently added.
Knowledge Connections
Discovers hidden patterns across your data — recurring ideas, abandoned goals, topics you keep returning to — and makes them visible in plain language.
Conversational Interface
Ask questions about your own knowledge base. "What startup ideas have I mentioned?" "What goals have I ignored?" Answers grounded in your actual documents.
Knowledge Feed
A scrollable stream of insights, connections, reminders, and ideas — surfaced continuously from your data, not from the internet.
Multi-source Ingestion
Connects to Google Drive, Gmail, Notion, Obsidian, journals, and uploaded documents. One unified knowledge base from all your existing tools.

Design principles
Calm
No notification badges, streaks, or urgency mechanics. Knowledge surfaces gently.
Thoughtful
Every surfaced item explains why it matters today. No raw data dumps.
Minimal
Avoids productivity-tool aesthetics. No cluttered dashboards or feature overload.
Personal
Grounded entirely in your data. Elio has no opinions — only your patterns.
Reflective
Designed to help you think, not to think for you. A companion, not a manager.
